Risk Mitigation Strategy

🎯 Challenge: Limited data for rare vehicles

Solution: Initial focus on high-volume models (Toyota, Nissan, Honda) with phased expansion as data accumulates

🎯 Challenge: Building user trust

Solution: Strategic partnerships with established dealerships, transparency in methodology, and free trial period

🎯 Challenge: Data acquisition and quality

Solution: Collaboration with automotive marketplaces and implementation of data validation protocols
